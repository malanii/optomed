Scope of services provided

Performing optometric tests and specific ophthalmic tests.

Conducting exercises in amblyopia and disorders binocular vision.

Selection of optical and non-optical aids.

Carrying out activities in the field of optometric and ophthalmic prophylaxis.

As an experienced optometrist, I can provide my patients with professional and comprehensive care, which is not limited only to physical health - by supervising it, because it is also necessary to take care of the patient's psyche, the condition of which directly affects the effectiveness of treatment.

We perform eye examinations In cases where there are difficulties related to binocular functioning, and exercises when the eyesight, due to the lack of proper cooperation of both eyeballs, is aillng.
